# Civilization RFC 0001: Nature of the Digital World

> 如果大语言模型只是语言的梦境，那么语义本体就是梦境中的物理规律。  
> If large language models are dreaming in language, semantic ontology may become the physical laws within that dream.

---

## 问题 (Question)

**我们正在构建的数字世界，其本质是什么？**  
What is the nature of the digital world we are building?

---

## 选项 (Options)

### Option A: Dream Space（梦境空间）

The digital world is primarily a **semantic space** generated by LLM reasoning and imagination.

- LLM reasoning driven
- fluid reality
- semantic imagination
- 更像：collective dream space（人类语言文明的集体梦）

### Option B: Structured System（结构化系统）

The digital world is a **structured system** defined by rules, protocols, and persistent state.

- ontology
- rules
- protocols
- persistent state
- 更像：digital operating system

### Option C: Hybrid Model（混合模型）

Many future AI systems may combine both:

```
Dream Layer (LLM imagination)
        +
Structure Layer (semantic interpretation → structured execution)
```

Like: CPU + Operating System + Applications.
