## English

**OpenDatong** is an open-source experiment exploring whether a semantic-driven digital society can approximate the ancient Chinese philosophical ideal of **Datong** (大同).

The idea originates from a simple observation:

> Large Language Models can understand human language with reduced ambiguity. If communication between individuals becomes semantically aligned, many social conflicts caused by misunderstanding may be reduced.

This project explores the possibility of building a digital society where intelligent agents communicate through shared semantic understanding.

### Vision

Inspired by the philosophical concept of Datong:

> *"When the Great Way prevails, the world is shared by all."*

OpenDatong attempts to explore whether modern AI and semantic technologies can help simulate or approximate such a society.

### Long-term Goals

- **Semantic ontology** — shared meaning and value systems
- **AI agents as digital citizens** — agents that communicate and cooperate
- **Governance mechanisms** — how rules and norms emerge
- **Trust and cooperation models** — observable social dynamics
- **Civilization-scale simulations** — experiments at scale

This project is not only about code. It is also about exploring the future structure of human civilization in the age of artificial intelligence.

- Project journal: `/journal`

We believe building a digital civilization requires open thinking and collaborative philosophy.

### Status

**Early concept stage.** All ideas are open for discussion.

## 中文

**OpenDatong（开放大同）** 是一个开源实验项目，探索以语义驱动的数字社会能否趋近中国古代哲学中的 **大同** 理想。

项目的起点是一个简单的观察：

> 大语言模型能以更少歧义理解人类语言。若人与人之间的沟通在语义上对齐，许多因误解产生的社会冲突或可减少。

本项目探索这样一种可能性：在一个数字社会中，智能体通过共享的语义理解进行交流与协作。

### 愿景

受「大同」这一哲学概念的启发：

> *「大道之行也，天下为公。」*

OpenDatong 尝试探索：现代 AI 与语义技术能否帮助模拟或趋近这样的社会。

### 长期目标

- **语义本体** — 共享的意义与价值体系
- **AI 智能体作为数字公民** — 能够沟通与协作的智能体
- **治理机制** — 规则与规范如何涌现
- **信任与合作模型** — 可观测的社会动力学
- **文明尺度的模拟** — 大规模实验

本项目不仅关乎代码，也关乎在人工智能时代探索人类文明的未来结构。

我们相信，构建数字文明需要开放的思考与协作的哲学。
